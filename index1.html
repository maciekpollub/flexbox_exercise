A dangerous metoeor is approaching the earth... Try to shoot it down and save the village.
The stage is a 300px-sqare. The position of the meteor is set randomly by the programm.
Guess the x (from between 0px and 270px) as well as y (from between 0px and 200px) coordinates, relating to the top-left corner of the stage and counting rightwise/downwise, respectively.
You have 5 takes.
